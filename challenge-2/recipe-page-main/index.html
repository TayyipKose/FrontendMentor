Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt,to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: Whisk eggs with salt and pepper. Add a bit of water or milk for fluffiness.
  2. Heat the pan: Warm a non-stick pan over medium heat. Add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the eggs: Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to spread evenly.
  4. Add fillings: When edges set and center is soft, add fillings on one half.
  5. Fold and serve: Fold the omelette, cook for 1 more minute, and slide onto a plate.
